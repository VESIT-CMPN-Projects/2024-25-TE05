Evacuation Measures for Cloud Burst

During a cloud burst, it's important to evacuate to safe locations. Below are recommendations on the types of places you should move to:

High Ground

Move to higher elevations away from low-lying areas to avoid flooding.

Reinforced Shelters

Seek out sturdy buildings with reinforced structures for maximum safety.

Safe Zones Marked by Authorities

Follow instructions from local authorities and evacuate to designated safe zones.

Avoid River Banks

Avoid areas near rivers and streams as they can flood rapidly during a cloud burst.

Away from Landslide-Prone Areas

Move away from hillsides and regions susceptible to landslides or rockfalls.

Stay Indoors During Rain

Stay indoors and avoid going outside during heavy rainfall to minimize risks.
